2. **When to choose what** — clear guidance on when to use milestone vs epic vs module vs task vs issue
3. **Order and reasons** — the hierarchy has a RATIONALE, not just levels
4. **Cases and examples** — real examples of each level in use
5. **Full custom parameters** — frontmatter fields, metadata blocks for each level
6. **Impediment types** — structured categories for what blocks work (not just "blocked")
