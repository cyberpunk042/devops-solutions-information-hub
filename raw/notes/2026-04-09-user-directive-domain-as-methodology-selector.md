# User Directive — 2026-04-09 — Domain as Methodology Selector

## Verbatim

> continue. i guess domain is one of the thing we use for flexibility and different methodology and chain patterns.

## Interpretation

The `domain` field isn't just for categorization — it's a condition for methodology model selection. Different domains can have different methodology chains, stage sequences, and artifact requirements. This connects to the Methodology Framework's "model selection per condition" concept. Domain is one of the selection dimensions alongside task_type, scale, and project phase.
