# Dual Perspective Directive — 2026-04-12

## Operator Directive (verbatim)

> "lets not forget the principle of things being directed toward this second-brain from the other project pespective and directed toward their own wiki too."

## Requirement

Everything we build must work from TWO perspectives:
1. A project looking AT the second brain (querying methodology, standards, chains, lessons)
2. A project looking at ITS OWN wiki (applying the methodology locally, tracking its own backlog, running its own pipeline)

The gateway tools, the identity protocol, the SDLC chains — all must serve both directions. The second brain is not a silo the project visits. It's a SERVICE the project integrates with while maintaining its own knowledge base.
