# E020 — Knowledge Sweep — Global Quality Confirmation by Human Review
## Summary

Global iteration pass — the operator reviews and confirms quality on ALL validated lessons (40), patterns (15), principles (3), decisions (17), and comparisons (6). Each page is checked against its per-type standard. Pages that don't meet the bar are flagged for rework. Pages that exceed it are promoted to canonical maturity (operator-gated). This is the 'iterate again and improve on everything multiple times' the operator asked for.

## Operator Directive

> "we need to iterate again and improve on everything multiple times"

> "Preach by example"

> "eventual global sweep of every lessons and modals and standards and examples and configs and human confirmations of them"
